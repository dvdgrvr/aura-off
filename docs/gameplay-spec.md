# Gameplay Spec

## Core loop
1. Enter arena
2. Position for pressure
3. Hold to charge aura
4. Survive crowd and hazard pressure
5. Avoid breaking
6. Release for payoff
7. Get summary
8. Restart instantly

## Design formula
The game should combine:
- core skill/tension
- crowd pressure
- social interference later
- occasional rare chaos

Target balance:
- 70% skill/tension
- 20% social sabotage
- 10% rare chaos

## Pressure sources
- nearby NPCs
- crowd attention
- hazard exposure
- risky arena zones
- later: player sabotage

## Hazard philosophy
Hazards should support the aura loop, not replace it.

Hazard types:
- core pressure hazards
- social sabotage hazards later
- rare chaos hazards

Rare unfair events are allowed if:
- they are memorable
- they are infrequent
- they are visually dramatic
- they do not dominate every round

## Tone
The game should take absurd moments seriously.
Funny events should feel dramatic and public.

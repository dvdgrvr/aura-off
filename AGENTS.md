## Hazard philosophy
Hazards are a supported part of the core design.

Use hazard categories:
- core_pressure
- social_sabotage
- chaos

Core pressure hazards should be readable and fairly common.
Chaos hazards should be rare, dramatic, and funny.
Chaos should not dominate the game.

A small amount of unfairness is acceptable if it creates memorable moments and remains infrequent.

## Art / VFX philosophy
Early visuals should be:
- simple
- readable
- expressive
- easy to replace

Do not block implementation on polished art.
Use placeholder shapes and lightweight effects freely.

The most important visual qualities are:
- silhouette clarity
- aura readability
- reaction readability
- strong release moments
- funny break states

## Animation philosophy
Prioritize animations that improve gameplay readability:
- charging
- unstable warning
- breaking
- release
- NPC reactions
- hazard telegraphs

Prefer a small set of clear exaggerated animations over many low-value animations.
